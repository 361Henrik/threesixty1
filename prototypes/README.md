# threesixty1.com Visual Prototype

Open `threesixty1-visual-directions.html` to review three clickable directions for the next threesixty1.com positioning site.

## Objective

Make a Norwegian SMB CEO or founder-led leader think:

"Henrik understands the pressure I am under, has real operator experience, and can help us move from AI uncertainty to a safe working prototype."

## Directions

- **A. The Leadership Brief:** premium editorial, trust-led, research-backed, strongest for authority.
- **B. The AI Workforce:** application-led, capacity-focused, strongest for making the offer tangible.
- **C. The Operator Field Guide:** personal and practical, strongest for making Henrik feel like the person they want in the room.

## Included Interactions

- Norwegian and English language toggle.
- Research-backed source strip.
- AI Readiness Reality Check.
- AI-supported role selector.
- Bring-one-workflow mini-diagnostic.
- Clear path toward the 30-Day AI Transformation Sprint.
